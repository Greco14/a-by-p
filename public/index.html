Approved by Pablo, or A(by)P, is a

new model for the gallery world that

harnesses the energy and excitement

of the ephemeral exhibition moment

and that makes art and ideas

accessible to all. Giving artists the

opportunity to curate entire events

rather than solely exhibit their work,

each A(by)P Event will include a

programme of multisensory and

all-encompassing experiences in

which everyone can participate.

Working through ight and sound as

much as taste and touch,

incorporating visual artefacts and

installations, performances and

theatre, workshops and films,

gastronomy and symposium, A(by)P

will present art as event, art as

encounter, and artas adventure.
